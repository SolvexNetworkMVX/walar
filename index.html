About Walar Network

Walar Network, powered by its native token $WALAR, is a cutting-edge blockchain infrastructure developed in Sibiu, Romania. Our mission is to provide fast, secure, and low-cost value transfer for the Walar Organization, a private group dedicated to cybersecurity and privacy.

Built with sustainability in mind, Walar Network operates on low-cost, eco-friendly hardware like Raspberry Pi, achieving an impressive 1000 TPS (transactions per second) with a 2-minute block time and no confirmations required.
